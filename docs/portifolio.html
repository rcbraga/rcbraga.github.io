PHAARM MatchMaker

Overview:

PHAARM MatchMaker is an innovative digital product that utilizes polypharmacology technology and artificial intelligence to bridge the gap between chemistry and biology in drug discovery.

Features & Benefits:

  • Establishes "molecular and biological signature" patterns, creating a comprehensive multi-biological profile of a drug's response to a disease.
  • Enhances therapy exploration, scaffold hopping, and preclinical toxicology prediction.
  • Facilitates pharmacokinetic comparison, anticipation of drug-drug interactions, and discovery of drug repurposing opportunities.

Overview:

RDrug is an AI-powered application designed to discover new therapeutic applications for drugs. It utilizes a proprietary "molecular and biological signatures" technology for drug repositioning.

Features & Benefits:

  • Helps to differentiate products from generics and protect them against competitors.
  • Accelerates development by an estimated 40% and reduces program duration by 1-3 years.

Detoxie for ICH M7

Overview:

Detoxie for ICH M7 is an AI-powered product designed for advanced mutagenicity and carcinogenicity risk assessment in the pharmaceutical industry. It evaluates a product's physicochemical and biopharmaceutical properties in line with ICH M7 guidelines.

Features & Benefits:

  • Predicts and assesses the mutagenic potential of drug impurities to facilitate compliance with ICH M7 regulations.
  • Aligns with international acceptance standards recommended by the OECD, ensuring reliability and security for technical-scientific reports.

ReactorSim

Overview:

ReactorSim is an innovative suite of software solutions designed to transform the API manufacturing and pharmaceutical industries, providing robust analysis of nitrosamine formation and its potential mutagenic and carcinogenic implications.

Features & Benefits:

  • Offers quantitative assessment of N-nitrosamine and nitrosamide drug substance-related impurities (NDSRIs).
  • Utilizes advanced stoichiometric calculations and worst-case kinetic mathematical models for accurate product yield analysis.
  • Employs AI and image recognition technology for regulatory compliance by transforming images into editable molecular structures.
